About G.D INTER COLLEGE

Director: Gaurav Rai

G.D INTER COLLEGE is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence, holistic development, and ethical leadership. Our mission is to impart quality education through modern teaching methods, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a culturally vibrant campus environment.

  • Renowned for academic achievement and dedicated faculty.
  • Modern labs, digital classrooms, and extensive library resources.
  • Emphasis on extracurricular activities including sports and cultural events.
  • Strong focus on leadership, innovation, and community service.
  • Supportive and inclusive environment encouraging student growth.
